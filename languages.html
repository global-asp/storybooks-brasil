Languages

The following languages are currently included in the scope of the Storybooks Brasil project:

  • alemão
  • árabe
  • chinês
  • coreano
  • espanhol
  • francês
  • guarani
  • ianomâmi
  • italiano
  • japonês
  • neerlandês
  • polonês
  • português
  • tupi
  • ucraniano
We are always looking for translators! If you speak one of the above languages and would like to help us translate stories, please contact us at globalafricanstorybook [at] gmail [dot] com
