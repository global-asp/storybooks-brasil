اُنْظُرْ إِلَى الحَيَوَانَاتِ Veja os animais Mira a los animales

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Heba Tesheh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk

Mashael Muhanna

Arabic

Level 1

اُنْظُرْ إِلَى الحَيَوَانَاتِ.

Veja os animais.

Mira a los animales.

البَقَرَةُ تَخُورُ.

A vaca diz, "muuu."

La vaca dice, "Muu."

الجَدْيُ يَقولُ "مييه... ميه".

A cabra diz, "mé, mé."

La cabra dice, "Be, be."

الحِصَانُ يَصْهَلُ.

O cavalo diz. "iihhh."

El caballo dice, "Hin."

الخِنْزِيرُ يَنْخِرُ.

O porco diz, "ronc, ronc."

El cerdo dice, "Oink-oink."

الدَّجَاجَةُ تُقَرْقِرُ.

A galinha diz, "cocoricó."

La gallina dice, "Cló-cló."

الكَلْبُ يَنبَحُ.

O cachorro diz, "au, au."

El perro dice, "Guau."

المُزَارِعُ يَقُولُ "شششش".

O fazendeiro diz, "xiu!"

El granjero dice, "¡Chsss!"

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Heba Tesheh, Maaouia Haj Mabrouk
Read by: Mashael Muhanna
